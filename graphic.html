STOP & BLOCK

Two Chinese characters in the middle means "stop" in English. Trees, grasses and creatures grow up from the words, and they are freezed. The images and the words combined together to present the main idea--"STOP". Gray and black colors present Chinese ordinary calligraphy, but other realistic images present modern ideas. In sum, the whole picture mixes traditional and mordern images.

Learning

The classroom becomes the space, and eight planets in the solar system hang up in the classroom. The lightness from the sun focuses on the desk and point out the topic of learning. The fancy scenerio metaphors the potential of education. To be more specific, teaching and learning in the classroom can bring students to everywhre they want to go. Students even can go to the space through learning.

The Moment

The fancy city is undersea. A girl is looking at the traffic light and wait for crossing the street, although there are no cars on the street. Blue colors of sea and red traffic light are strong contrasts, so viewers can focus on the middle of the picture. The red light is a normal diea of stop, and it can bring viewers a strong impression of stop and wait.

Gender-Female

I use pink blocks to draw out a female's head with no face. Then viewer can figure out her gender only by pink color and hairstyle. I wan to critize the ideal type of female by the image of color and style.

Gender-Male

I want to use the image to criticize the concept that people recognize an individual in gender rather than a human being. Viwers can see the rough outline of a man, but they can figure out his gender only by colors and hairstyle. The situation reflect deep bias of gender.

I want to express my respect to the Umbrella Revolution in Hong Kong. The flat design makes the picture less serious but warmer. That's my impression of the movement.

Darlie Toothpaste
Logo Redesign

Darlie Toothpaste is a old brand in Taiwan. I did a brand redesign for the brand and made it more modern and fashion. I kept the core brand ideas of black and white and used those two colors as main visual design. Because most users are right-handed, I put the logo on the left side, therefore, users can see the logo very clearly when they use the toothpaste.

Raining-Typo

Those two Chinese words mean "Rains" in English. I changed some strokes with different front type and added dotted line to present the abstract idea of rain. I also used water color on the words to made them like sinked in the water.

Jump
Name Card Design

This is my personal name card. There are two layers with one striped layer. If you move the striped layer from left to right, you will see the man jump from left to right. I like the graphic deisgn works that can interact with others, the name card can represent my interest and the spirit of crossing each difficulty.
